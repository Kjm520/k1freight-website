Who We Are

Precision Over Volume.

K1 Freight is a dual-authority freight agency built on Ryder's transportation infrastructure, one of the most trusted names in logistics. We are not a high-volume clearing house. We are a focused, precision operation that treats every load like the critical asset it is.

We work with power companies, industrial operators, and project managers whose freight doesn't allow for second chances. Rotors, turbine components, oversized structural pieces, and time-sensitive industrial equipment: these are our specialty. If your shipment is preventing a power plant shutdown or restoring power for an entire region, that's not the kind of work you hand to the lowest bidder.

When you call K1 Freight, someone with the knowledge and authority to act picks up. That's not a standard at most brokerages. It's our foundation.
